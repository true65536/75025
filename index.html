Use your arrow keys or WASD (on computer) or swipe (on mobile) to move the tiles. When two neighboring numbers from the Fibonacci sequence touch, they merge into the next one!
The Fibonacci sequence starts 1, 2 and to get the next number in the sequence, add the previous two together. Try to get 75025. It's possible.

Based on TheReal23's 2584 game, which in turn was based on cozmic72's 987 game, which in turn was based on 2048, created by Gabriele Cirulli, which was in turn based on 1024 by Veewo Studio, which was in turn based on Threes by Asher Vollmer. The adaptation to '75025' and the Fibonacci sequence is by Jonathan Ross. Colors, font, and goal tweaked by true65536.
